How to Use:

  1. Allow Location Access: The app needs your location to track satellites overhead.
  2. View the Map: Your location will be marked with a blue pin.
  3. Satellite Markers: Satellites currently in space will appear as red markers on the map.
  4. Notifications: When a satellite flies directly over you, you'll see a notification at the top!
  5. Auto-Update: The satellite positions update every 30 seconds automatically.

Tip: Click on any satellite marker to see its name and details!
